Animals



kittens

kittens are amazing fluffy creatures
they are also young, playful
, and highly dependent felines that require
warmth, nutrition, and care

Hamsters

Hamsters are popular small rodents
known for their expandable cheek pouches and nocturnal activity.
There are five main species kept as pets,
each with unique characteristics and care requirements,
especially regarding housing them alone or in pairs.

puppies

Puppies are young dogs, known for being playful and energetic,
requiring significant care, training, and socialization,
especially between 8-12 weeks old, to develop
properly and avoid fearfulness as adults.
